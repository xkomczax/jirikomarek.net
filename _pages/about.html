Hello,
My name is Jiri Komarek, and I am a landscape photographer from the Czech Republic.

I grew up in the heart of Drahanská highland, where I was always close to nature.
It formed my main photographic topic.

For me, photography is not just a way to show the beauty of nature but also a way to point out the importance of protecting our fragile ecosystem.
